# Reading Tracker Pages

## Pages and Purpose

- Home page: The first page users see when visiting the website; it does not require login and allows anyone to browse books, see average star ratings, and view like counts.
- Login/Signup page: Allows users to create an account or sign in so they can like books, rate with stars, and post comments.
- Book detail page: Shows full book information and community activity, including comments, individual ratings, and like actions.
- Profile page: Shows a user's account details and their activity, such as comments posted, books liked, and ratings given.
- Bookshelves page: Displays a user's different bookshelves or reading lists such as Read, Currently Reading, To Be Read, Did Not Finish.
